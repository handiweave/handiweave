Our Story

Handiweave was born in the picturesque hills of Palampur, Himachal Pradesh, with a mission to preserve and promote traditional weaving techniques passed down through generations.

Each product is meticulously handcrafted by skilled artisans who pour their heart and soul into every weave. We work directly with these artisans, ensuring fair wages and sustainable livelihoods.

Our materials are sourced locally - from the finest wool of Himalayan sheep to luxurious angora. Every piece is a blend of traditional craftsmanship and contemporary design.

When you buy from Handiweave, you're not just purchasing a product; you're supporting a community, preserving a heritage, and owning a piece of authentic Himachali craftsmanship.
