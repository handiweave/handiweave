A Soul-Wrapped Heritage

This is an effort to bring a hidden craft into the light — not just to be seen, but to be felt. Our mission is to empower the hands behind the loom, and to honour their quiet strength. Each tana-bana carries a heartbeat — of tradition, of memory, of love passed down generations.

We don’t just weave shawls; we preserve stories that might otherwise fade into silence. Reviving heritage, we give it a direction that belongs to tomorrow.

Every piece sustains a life, a family, a village — and keeps the local economy gently flowing. As the tana-bana grows, so does a legacy rooted in dignity and care. This is not a product. It’s a soul-wrapped heritage.
